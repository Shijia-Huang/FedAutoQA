CDORS – HCUP Central Distributor Online Reporting System

General Description:
The HCUP Central Distributor Online Reporting System (CDORS), accessible via https://cdors.ahrq.gov/, is AHRQ’s official portal for securely ordering, purchasing, and managing Healthcare Cost and Utilization Project (HCUP) datasets. It is operated by TurningPoint-DSFederal JV LLC under AHRQ contract HHSM500201600039I, with payment processing handled by NORC at the University of Chicago  [oai_citation:0‡cdors.ahrq.gov](https://cdors.ahrq.gov/special-pages/my-account?utm_source=chatgpt.com).

• User registration with HCUP Data Use Agreement (DUA) training verification  [oai_citation:1‡distributor.hcup-us.ahrq.gov](https://www.distributor.hcup-us.ahrq.gov/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
• Browse national and state HCUP databases; order supplemental files (e.g., CCR, AHA linkage, HMS, trend weights)  [oai_citation:2‡distributor.hcup-us.ahrq.gov](https://www.distributor.hcup-us.ahrq.gov/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
• Mandatory acceptance of DUAs, purchaser responsibilities, indemnity clauses; Statement of Intended Use required for state-level data  [oai_citation:3‡distributor.hcup-us.ahrq.gov](https://www.distributor.hcup-us.ahrq.gov/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)  
• Online purchase workflow: add to cart, legal review, billing/shipping input, payment via credit card, ACH, wire, or purchase order ()

Typical Workflow:
1. Register & complete DUA training  
2. Select datasets & supplemental files  
3. Complete legal agreements & SIU (for state data)  
4. Provide billing/shipping/student verification (as needed)  
5. Submit payment  
6. Download national data or receive state data via physical shipment  
7. Comply with reuse, custody transfer, or publication rules
